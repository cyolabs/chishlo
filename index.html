Join our community of families with children with hearing loss to discover the benefits of family-to-family support and tap into the collective wisdom of others on similar journeys.

How your donation helps deaf children

Thanks to people like you, we can continue to make a difference. Your donation to Children Speech and Hearing Loss Organisation helps us to share knowledge with families, offer essential family-to-family support, and represent their perspectives in research and program development. Your contribution is essential in assisting deaf children and their families to thrive.

We couldn’t do what we do without the support of people like you.
